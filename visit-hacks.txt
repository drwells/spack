1. create a symbolic link libhdf5_mpi.so to libhdf5.so
2. vtk should be compiled with -DBUILD_ALL_MODULES=ON
3. visit depends on vtk + qt:
    depends_on('vtk@6.1.0~opengl2~mpi+qt')
4. compile VTK with BUILD_ALL_MODULES=ON (this may not be necessary)
5. visit depends on the newest QT instead of an old one
6. visit depends on hdf5+mpi when +hdf5 and +mpi are given
7. add flags -DVISIT_QT5=ON, -DVISIT_QT_DIR=/qt/prefix to VISIT
8. set the correct QMAKE exe: qmake-qt5
